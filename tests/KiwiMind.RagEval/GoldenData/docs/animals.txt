The African elephant is the largest land animal on Earth, weighing up to 6,000 kilograms and using its long trunk to grasp food and water.

The cheetah is the fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds over 100 kilometers per hour in short bursts while hunting.

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals that communicate using clicks and whistles, and they live in social groups called pods.

The Arctic tern holds the record for the longest annual migration of any animal, traveling from the Arctic to the Antarctic and back each year.

Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood, and they are known for their remarkable problem-solving abilities and camouflage skills.
